ABOUT ME

First and foremost, I never thought I'd love application development as much as I do now. I love the fact that web development is constantly evolving and in order to maintain relevancy in the field, you have to evolve with it. As a Full Stack Web Developer, I am passionate, determined, and flowing with creativity. I love working with a diverse team in effort to achieve a shared goal. Team members come from different walks of life and more specifically different technology environments; in this fashion, I am able to pick everyone's brain in effort to improve my technology stack and creative focus.
Outside of coding, I love to enjoy myself (let's be honest, there's nothing better than enjoying yourself). You can find me dancing the night away, love meeting new people, and/or traveling across seas to uncharted lands. Secondly, I believe the greatest form of wealth is by having good mental and physical health. Coding is a challenge and I believe a healthy state of mind is required in order to tackle it head on. Lastly, but most important, I am a family man; family is everything.
